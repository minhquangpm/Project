Tables

Poliform

"Designer tables reflects on all aspects of daily life, through each space in the home and office, bringing together high quality materials and distinguishing features in an indissoluble combination of efficiency, stability and elegance. The range explores and surpasses the classic schemes of architectural design, elevating exclusive materials such as marble, solid wood and pure crystal into simplified shapes, sculptural volumes and geometrical lines. Slender legs and minimalist bases give way to advanced solutions and complex shapes that even include a frame of twisted slats, an intersection of stretched cables conceived as a tensegrity structure, and immovable supports creating monolithic fluting or a unique play on solids and voids."

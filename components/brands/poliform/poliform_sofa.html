Sofas

Poliform

"Designer sofas epitomize the brilliance achieved by the company in the upholstered furniture industry in over 90 years of history - a model of beauty and harmony that brings together craftsmanship and innovation in the spirit of avant-gardism. Structures reveal precise, engineered mechanisms, developed to accompany every gesture and movement with dynamism and equilibrium, and rendered in both linear and modular constructions. The exclusive upholstery includes premium quality full grain leather, high-class saddle leather and sleek sartorial fabrics, completed with signature double-stitching and needlecraft to infuse the Meda Made savoir faire into every surface."

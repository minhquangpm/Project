Beds

Cassina

"Modern bedrooms, havens of relaxation, favour designer beds for their smooth, comfortable elegance, featuring streamlined shapes and well-balanced volumes that heighten the feeling of lightness and pleasure. Designed to coordinate with the matching range of sofas, these unique bed frames include fixed die-cast aluminium constructions and flexible structures, perfectly in harmony with the wide range of low tables and stools available in the collection.

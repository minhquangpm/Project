Beds

Poliform

"Modern bedrooms, havens of relaxation, favour designer beds for their smooth, comfortable elegance, featuring streamlined shapes and well-balanced volumes that heighten the feeling of lightness and pleasure. Designed to coordinate with the matching range of sofas, these unique bed frames include fixed die-cast aluminium constructions and flexible structures, perfectly in harmony with the wide range of low tables and stools available in the collection. Shapes are made up of slender, minimalist lines, polyurethane padding and headboards with soft cushions enhanced with multiple layers of foam and down."

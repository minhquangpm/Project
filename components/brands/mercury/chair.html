Chairs

Poliform

"This extensive range of designer chairs is conceived for a variety of uses and furnishing styles, from home settings to work and representative spaces. The evolution of the icons of the great masters of architecture - reissued with absolute respect for the authentic pieces - meets with the new shapes of contemporaneity, from clean-cut geometries and minimalist proportions to smooth and sinuous shapes. In a continuous balance of stability and lightness, high comfort merges with meticulous attention to the most exclusive details and executions, revealing perfect stitching on fine leathers and creative constructions in wood or aluminium. "
